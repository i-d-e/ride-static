RIDE provides the gathered data under CC BY licence. The reviews and
questionnaires are available in XML/TEI and as PDF on
[GitHub](https://github.com/i-d-e/ride) and
[Zenodo](https://zenodo.org/record/4562966); the latter allows for
citing the data in a specific version with an own DOI. Retrieval and
re-use of the review metadata is also supported through an
[OAI-interface](/data/).
