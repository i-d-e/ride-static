RIDE is constantly calling for [reviews](/call-for-reviews/) on
digital scholarly editions, digital text collections and digital
tools and environments for digital scholarly editing.

Would you like to contribute a review to RIDE? You can either suggest
to review a project of your own choosing or
[look up our list](/projects-for-review/) of projects that need
reviewing.
