# About RIDE

RIDE — Reviews in Digital Editions — is a peer-reviewed journal devoted
to scholarly digital editions. Each review evaluates a digital edition
against a structured set of criteria established by the
*Institut für Dokumentologie und Editorik* (IDE) and is published
under a Creative Commons licence.
