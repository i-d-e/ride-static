# About RIDE

RIDE is an [open access](/publishing-policy/) review journal dedicated
to digital editions and resources. RIDE aims to direct attention to
digital editions and to provide a forum in which expert peers
criticise and discuss the efforts of digital editors in order to
improve current practices and advance future developments. It does so
by asking its reviewers to pay attention not only to the traditional
virtues and vices of any edition, but also to the progressing
methodology and its technical implications. Read more about RIDE in
our [editorial](/editorial/).

## Forthcoming issues

If you are interested in contributing a review,
[please contact the editors](/call-for-reviews/) beforehand. We
maintain a [list of suggested projects](/projects-for-review/) we
would like to see reviews of, but we are also happy to consider your
suggestions. Please contact the editors in either case before
beginning to work on your review.

## Team

RIDE is published by the
[Institute for Documentology and Scholarly Editing](https://www.i-d-e.de)
(IDE). Details about the editorial board and the technical
realisation can be found on our [team page](/team/).
