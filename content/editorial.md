# Editorial

RIDE — Reviews in Digital Editions and Resources — was launched by
the *Institute for Documentology and Scholarly Editing* (IDE) to give
scholarly digital editions the kind of structured, rigorous review
that traditional editions have always received in print. Each issue
addresses a thematic focus or a particular edition type, and every
review is written against the IDE criteria for reviewing digital
editions.

The journal foregrounds the methodological and technical dimensions
of digital editing. Reviewers report not only on the philological and
editorial merits of an edition but also on its data model, its
encoding decisions, its long-term sustainability, and its accessibility
to scholars and the wider public. The result is a body of literature
that is itself a record of how the field is changing.
