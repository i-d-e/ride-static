# Editorial

RIDE — Reviews in Digital Editions — was launched by the *Institut für
Dokumentologie und Editorik* (IDE) to give scholarly digital editions
the kind of structured, rigorous review they deserve. Each issue
addresses a thematic focus or a particular edition type, and every
review is written against the IDE criteria for reviewing digital
editions.
