# Questionnaires

Every RIDE review answers a structured questionnaire of criteria,
either the *Criteria for Reviewing Digital Editions* or the *Criteria
for Reviewing Tools and Environments*. Each criterion is answered on a
three-point scale (Yes / No / Not Applicable, encoded as `1`, `0`,
`2`).
