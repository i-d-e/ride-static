# Publishing Policy

All reviews published in RIDE are peer-reviewed and released under a
Creative Commons licence so they can be cited, archived, and reused
without barrier. The XML source of every review is published alongside
the rendered text, and a structured questionnaire records the reviewer's
answers to the IDE criteria.
