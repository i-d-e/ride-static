# Projects for Review

We are very interested in reviews of the following projects (but of course you can also suggest a project that is not on this list). If you would like to contribute to RIDE, please contact the managing editors at ride-editor (at) i-d-e.de. For projects that are already under review, [see here](http://ride.i-d-e.de/reviewers/projects-currently-under-review/).
