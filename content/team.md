## Editorial Boards per Issue

RIDE is published by the
[Institute for Documentology and Scholarly Editing](https://www.i-d-e.de)
(IDE). Each issue is organised by guest editors together with the
managing editors; the full editorial board for any given issue is
listed on its [issue page](/issues/).

## Technical Realization

The published reviews are encoded in TEI / XML. The site is built
from the TEI corpus by a Python rendering pipeline that consumes the
TEI source, the editorial Markdown maintained alongside it, and the
per-issue YAML configuration. Cross-references, factsheets, and
citation exports are generated at build time. Source code, schemas,
and tooling are open-source and available on the
[RIDE GitHub organisation](https://github.com/i-d-e).
