# Call for Reviews

RIDE is constantly calling for reviews on

- [scholarly digital editions](https://ride.i-d-e.de/reviewers/call-for-reviews/scholarly-editions/)

- [digital text collections](https://ride.i-d-e.de/reviewers/call-for-reviews/special-issue-text-collections/)

- [tools and environments for scholarly editing](https://ride.i-d-e.de/reviewers/call-for-reviews/tools-and-environments-for-digital-scholarly-editing/)

and related digital resources.

We are particularly interested in reviews of some projects (see [Suggested Projects for Review](http://ride.i-d-e.de/reviewers/suggested-projects-for-review/)). Please contact the [managing editors](https://ride.i-d-e.de/about/newsletter/) if you would like to volunteer to review a project from the list or of your own choosing and include the project, your name and the institution you work for, as well as information regarding your qualification in the email. Please also check page with [Projects Currently under Review](http://ride.i-d-e.de/about/projects-currently-under-review/) for projects that have already been assigned reviewers.

Publications will be licensed under CC-BY. RIDE does not charge submission, processing or publication fees of any sort.

## **Special Issues**

A special issue focusing on **Scholarly Digital Editions with a connection to Switzerland** has been active since April 2026. For more information, see the [Call for Reviews](https://ride.i-d-e.de/reviewers/call-for-reviews/swiss-editions-en/). Contributions are welcome.

A special issue focusing on **Digital Scholarly Editions of Dramatic Texts and Related Theatrical Materials** has been active since June 2025. For more information, see the [Call for Reviews](https://ride.i-d-e.de/reviewers/call-for-reviews/drama-editions-en/) (also available in [German](https://ride.i-d-e.de/reviewers/call-for-reviews/drama-editions-de/)). Contributions are welcome.

Since October 2023, a special issue has been active on **Crowd-Sourced Scholarly Editions**. See the [Call for Reviews](https://ride.i-d-e.de/reviewers/call-for-reviews/crowd-editions-en/) (also available in [German](https://ride.i-d-e.de/reviewers/call-for-reviews/crowd-editions-de/)) and the list of [Suggested Projects for Review](https://ride.i-d-e.de/reviewers/call-for-reviews/crowd-editions/). Contributions are welcome.
